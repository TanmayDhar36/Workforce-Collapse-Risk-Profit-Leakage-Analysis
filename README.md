## 🔍 Overview
This project is a **People Analytics case study** analyzing workforce data of **1,000 employees** across multiple dimensions to uncover hidden business risks.

The analysis focuses on:
- Attrition Risk
- Burnout & Engagement
- Compensation vs Performance
- Business Unit Profitability
- External Market & Automation Risk

---

## 📁 Dataset Details
- 7 Data Sheets  
- 56 Variables  
- Covers:
  - Employee Demographics  
  - Compensation  
  - Performance Metrics  
  - Engagement & Behavior  
  - Attrition Risk  
  - Business Impact  
  - External Market Factors  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- IT Business Unit shows **highest cost but lowest profit**
- **27.9% employees at high attrition risk**
- **29.2% workforce critically burned out**
- **37.6% employees disengaged**
- Salary vs Performance correlation ≈ **0 (0.011)**

---

## ⚠️ Business Impact

- Profit leakage due to inefficient cost structure  
- High workforce instability risk  
- Weak performance-reward alignment  
- Increasing burnout affecting productivity  

---

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

- Targeted retention strategy for high-risk employees  
- Burnout reduction initiatives (workload & policy changes)  
- Compensation restructuring based on performance  
- AI-driven reskilling & workforce planning

## 📌 Key Takeaway

Workforce issues are not just HR concerns —  
they are **direct drivers of business performance and profitability**.

Organizations often face gradual decline due to:
- Misaligned compensation  
- Rising burnout  
- Hidden attrition risk  

Early detection through data is critical.
