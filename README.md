📊 Workforce Collapse Risk & Profit Leakage Analysis
🔍 Project Overview

This project analyzes workforce data of 1,000 employees to uncover hidden risks impacting business performance, including:

Attrition risk
Burnout and engagement
Compensation misalignment
Profit leakage across business units
📁 Dataset
7 data sheets
56 variables
Covers: employee data, performance, compensation, engagement, and external market
📈 Key Insights
IT unit shows highest cost but lowest profit
27.9% employees at high attrition risk
29.2% workforce critically burned out
37.6% disengaged employees
Salary vs performance correlation: ~0 (0.011)
⚠️ Business Impact
Profit leakage due to inefficiency
High-risk workforce instability
Weak performance-reward linkage
💡 Recommendations
Targeted retention strategy
Burnout reduction initiatives
Compensation restructuring
AI-driven reskilling programs
